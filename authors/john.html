John Hoffman

John Hoffman is the author of Oil Spill. He has also written for Maximum Rocknroll, Revolver, and Cvlt Nation, and has been involved in the punk, hardcore, and metal scenes since 1998. John has toured around the world as a vocalist for the hardcore band Weekend Nachos, and currently self-produces the doom metal band, Stomach.

John lives in Geneva, Illinois, a suburb of Chicago, with his wife Destynne and daughters Grace and Lillian. Besides writing and playing music, John enjoys all types of food and video games from the 80s and 90s.
