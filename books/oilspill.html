Oil Spill

By John Hoffman

About the Book

Oil Spill is the first collection of short stories from John Hoffman, vocalist of the hardcore/punk band Weekend Nachos. This collection features six (6) fictional tales that focus on both the atrocities of human nature as well as dark, fantastical themes of horror and suspense. Perfect for fans of Stephen King and The X-Files.
